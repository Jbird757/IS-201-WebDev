Super Store Tableau

  • This map of the US shows total profits by state. California and New York are the states with the most profit and Texas is the state with the biggest deficit. Profits are positive across the 50 states, despite there being an even number of profitable states to the number of deficit states, because the profit states are bringing in much more profit than we are losing to the deficit states.

  • This graph shows the profit breakdown of New York, one of the most profitable states, by category. Technology is the prominent category here, outperforming office supplies by double and furniture by 7 times.

  • This graph shows the profit of New York during the past two years. Notice the big spike in profit during fall 2014. Before then we saw regular spikes, but this big spike shows that we did something right during that time.

  • This graph shows the sales and the forecast of sales for the next year in New York state. There is some information missing, which is shown by the breaks in the line. Overall, both the profits and sales reflect that New York is growing in profitability and probably will continue to do so over the next year.
